About Us

Imisebe is a licensed internet and telephone services provider. We offer voice and data services to business and homes in South Africa. Operating since 2013, Imisebe is known for building, monitoring, operating and maintaining one of the most advanced Fixed Wireless networks in South Africa. The network and equipment are fully owned and managed by Imisebe Telecoms, and we connect directly to the world via undersea cable. The company prides itself in being the first fixed wireless provider to offer affordable uncapped wireless at speeds of up to 50 Mbps on a point-to-multipoint connection. Management’s hands-on approach and daily involvement ensure that Imisebe Telecoms continuously develop new solutions and adapt the network as industry, customer demands change, and technology advances. Our staff’s commitment to and love for the business ensure that you always receive exceptional and personal service.

Our Services

We provide a range of service

Voice Over IP Land Line System

VOIP is an acronym for Voice over Internet Protocol, or in more common terms Phone Service over the Internet. If you have our Internet connection, you can get phone service delivered through your Internet connection instead of legendary Landline. We can port (transfer ownership of the number to you) your numbers, saving you money on line charges off your current bill. Move just your primary number or all of your numbers. In VoIP terms, a number is a caller channel through which you can make multiple calls. Unlike a telco fixed line where there is only one call per line, here we have as many calls as your PABX can handle through your internet connection.

Hosting

Email : Private or business email hosting with or without your own customised domain name. (person@yourbusiness.co.za).

Website : We offer Linux website hosting on our fully redundant server and network infrastructure.

Virtual Private Network (VPN)(L2/L3)

A virtual private network extends a private network across our network, and enables your business to have its own private WAN in our network and you can connect your entire branches as one office.

Rack space

Host your own hardware in one of our secure local or international data centres with fibre backbone, fire suppression, redundant power supply and high physical security.

Wireless Internet Connection up to 1Gbps

Imisebe Telecoms has built wireless access network throughout South Africa, enabling home and business customers to experience good speeds with fibre backbone network for reliability at a fraction of the cost. Real speeds of up to 1Gbps can be realised for enterprise customers eliminating the need to wait for fibre especially in remote areas.
